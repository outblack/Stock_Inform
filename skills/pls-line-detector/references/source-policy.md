# Source Policy

Use this file whenever `pls-line-detector` is active.

## Priority Order

Use sources in this order when possible:

1. reliable market-data pages for long-term price history, drawdown context, and volume behavior
2. company disclosures and official releases for catalyst survival
3. reputable business news for narrative continuity or renewed expectation
4. charting pages for weekly and monthly support-resistance confirmation

## Verification Rules

Before calling something a valid PLS setup, try to confirm:

- the stock is materially below peak
- volume and attention have cooled
- the expectation is not completely dead
- weekly or monthly reaction zones repeat enough to matter

If any of these are weak, say so directly.
