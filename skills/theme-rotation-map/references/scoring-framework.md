# Scoring Framework

Use this file to rank themes consistently.

## Three Core Scores

Score each theme on a 1 to 5 scale for:

- `Continuity`: is the move sustaining across sessions, breadth, or repeated catalysts
- `Expectation`: is there still a believable future catalyst or narrative expansion left
- `Freshness`: does the theme still feel early and underexplored rather than fully crowded

## Suggested Reading Of Scores

- `5`: very strong
- `4`: strong
- `3`: mixed but alive
- `2`: weak
- `1`: nearly dead

## Ranking Guidance

Prioritize:

- strong continuity plus strong expectation
- strong freshness only when the tape confirms participation
- themes with credible leaders rather than purely narrative noise

Be cautious with themes that score high on freshness but low on turnover or breadth.

## Rotation Guidance

For each top theme, outline:

- direct continuation scenario
- relay into secondary names
- failure or exhaustion scenario

Keep the scenarios conditional and evidence-based.
